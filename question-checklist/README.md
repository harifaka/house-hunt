\# Question List Guide \& Checklist



Welcome to the \*\*Question List Guide \& Checklist\*\*! This folder contains a carefully curated list of essential questions and things to check before purchasing a home. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or experienced in the real estate market, this guide will ensure you don’t overlook any crucial details during your house hunt.



\## Features



\### ✅ Essential Questions to Ask Before Buying a Home

This document provides a comprehensive list of questions to ask real estate agents, sellers, and yourself. It covers a wide range of topics such as:



\- \*\*Property Condition:\*\* Questions about the roof, foundation, plumbing, and more.

\- \*\*Neighborhood and Location:\*\* Inquiries about the local community, schools, safety, and amenities.

\- \*\*Legal and Financial:\*\* Things to consider about taxes, property liens, and the overall affordability of the home.

\- \*\*Homeowner’s Association (HOA):\*\* If applicable, what to ask about HOA fees, rules, and restrictions.



\### 📝 House Inspection Checklist

A detailed checklist that helps you keep track of the condition of the house during an inspection. This includes:



\- \*\*Exterior and Interior:\*\* Assessing the structural condition, appliances, windows, and doors.

\- \*\*Utilities and Systems:\*\* Ensuring that electrical, HVAC, plumbing, and other systems are in working order.

\- \*\*Safety Features:\*\* Checking smoke detectors, fire escapes, and any other safety equipment.



---



\## How to Use



1\. \*\*Review the Questions:\*\* Start by reading through the questions list to get an understanding of the key areas to consider when viewing a home.

2\. \*\*Take Notes:\*\* During home tours, jot down your thoughts, answers, and any concerns that arise in the provided space.

3\. \*\*Evaluate the Answers:\*\* After receiving answers from sellers, agents, or through your own research, evaluate how they align with your needs and expectations.
